User Guide

Brewery Managers

Company Inventory
Click "McInventory" and select "Total Inventory" from the menu. Data will display as a graph or table.
Change your data preference below the display.
Select "Search Inventory" under "Total Inventory" to look for a specific SKU or beer name and keg size.
Fill out the form on the left side including "Criteria" and "Key Word" and click "Search". Spelling and capitalization matter.
Search results display on the right with the option to go directly to the brewery home page.

Brewery Home Page
Click "Breweries" in the header and select your location from the menu.
Inventory data is first, followed by orders and the optional "Beer Coming Soon" table which is valuable for pub managers.
From this page inventory is controlled and orders can be approved, deleted and archived.

Orders
To approve, click "Update" and fill out the form on left. Change status, adjust quantity if needed and click "Submit".
The distribution manager needs all oders to be approved prior to scheduling delivery.
Click "Archive Order" below and left of each table to remove from home page.
To search orders, click "Search Archive" on the right and fill out the form on left. You can search by date, property or both.
Please note: spelling and capitalization matter.

Inventory Control
Click "Add/Update Inventory" on the top/right of page. Current inventory will display on the right and "Add Kegs" form on left.
To edit keg inventory, click "Update" for the target beer/size. Name, SKU and quantity are adjustable. Click "Submit" when done.
To add to inventory, fill out "Add Kegs" form on the left and click "Submit". All fields are required and spelling/capitalization matter.

Coming Soon
Click "Add/Update Upcoming" on the top/right of page. Current listings will be on the right and "Add Beer" form on left.
Fill out "Add Beer" form and click "Submit" to add listing. The "Finish Date" will allow pub managers to plan future orders.

Distribution Managers

Orders
From the "Departments" page, click "Distribution Admin" found below the sun logo. This will not be visable if you are not authorized.
The home page is "Orders". Tables will diplay recent and pending orders with cart, keg and order status on the right.
Update completed orders by clicking on "Order Delivered" on lower/left.
Click on "Archive Order" to remove from the order page.

Search Order Archive
Click "Orders" in header and select "Search Archive" in menu.
Fill out form on the left side. You can search by property, date or both. Select "All" for an entire year. Spelling/capitalization matter.
The results display in tables on the right.

Drivers

Routes
This section provides valuable information to the new driver or for a driver covering an unknown route.
Click "Routes" in the header and select "All Routes". Routes will be listed by truck number and day.
For specific results, select "Search Routes" and enter truck number and day into the form and click "Submit".
Click on the property code for detailed information including weather, picture, contacts, instructions and map.

Pub Managers

Place an Order
Click "Place Order" in header and select your assigned brewery from menu.
The table on the right side shows the current inventory and the last time it was updated.
Fill out the form on the left side. You can also include a request or message to the brewery.
Click "Add to Cart". The quantity will appear next to the cart. Continue to add kegs as needed.
Per brewery approval, kegs from multiple locations can be in one cart.
Look for "Beer Coming Soon" table at the bottom for info on the beer you may want, but is not yet available.
Click the shopping cart anytime to see order details. To adjust quantity delete item and re-enter.
Click "Submit Order" when finished. You can also include a message to the distribution manager.

Viewing Orders
Click "Orders" and select "Pending Orders" from menu.
Recent and pending orders will be displayed in tables with the keg and order status on the right.
Once the brewery had approved the kegs, the distribution manager will schedule delivery and update status.
After the order has been delivered, the distribution manager will archive the order removing it from here.

Search Order Archive
Click "Orders" and select "Order Archive" from menu.
Fill out the form on the left. Select "All" to search an entire year. Otherwise, select month and year and click "Search".
The results display in tables on the right.
